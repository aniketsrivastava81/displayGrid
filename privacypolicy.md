We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on Our Service and store certain information. Tracking technologies may include beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze Our Service.

The technologies We may use include:

- **Necessary Cookies:** required for core functionality such as security, authentication, and access to account features.
- **Preference Cookies:** used to remember settings and improve user experience.
- **Analytics Cookies:** used to understand how the Service is used and to improve performance.

You can instruct Your browser to refuse all Cookies or to indicate when a Cookie is being sent. However, if You do not accept Cookies, some parts of the Service may not function properly.
