# Delete Data Instructions

**Last updated:** March 19, 2026

If you would like your data deleted from **displayGrid**, you may request deletion at any time.

## How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data associated with displayGrid, please contact us by email at:

- **Email:** superfuelgreen@protonmail.com

Please include the following in your email:

- Your full name
- The email address associated with your account
- A clear statement that you want your data deleted

## What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon verification of your request, we will delete or disable access to the personal data we control that is associated with your displayGrid account, including where applicable:

- Your account profile information
- Your login and account-related records
- Connected social media integration data
- Stored authentication tokens or secure connection credentials used to retrieve approved media
- Images, media references, or cached application grid content associated with your account
- Support history or contact records, unless retention is required for legal or security reasons

## Social Media Connected Accounts

If you connected a social media account to displayGrid for retrieving images or populating the application grid in the About Us section of a webpage, we will remove or disable the connection and delete associated stored access credentials within a reasonable period after verifying your deletion request.

You may also independently revoke displayGrid’s access through your social media platform account settings.

## What May Be Retained

In certain cases, we may retain limited information where necessary to:

- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce agreements
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Maintain backup or security logs for a limited retention period

Where retention is required, we will retain only the minimum information necessary for those purposes.

## Processing Time

We aim to respond to verified deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe. In some cases, additional time may be required if we need to verify your identity or process data stored across backup or security systems.

## Account Deletion Through the App

If displayGrid provides an in-app account deletion option, you may also use that feature to request deletion of your data directly from within the application.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about deleting your data, contact us at:

- **Email:** superfuelgreen@protonmail.com
